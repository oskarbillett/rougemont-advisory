Strategic Services

Institutional-quality analytical support tailored for climate tech startups

Financial Modelling

Build sophisticated financial models that provide deep insights into your business economics. We create dynamic models covering revenue projections, unit economics, cash flow forecasting, and scenario analysis. Our models help you understand the key drivers of your business, optimize capital allocation, and build compelling investment cases that resonate with VCs. We specialize in climate tech-specific metrics and can model complex revenue streams including carbon credits, regulatory incentives, and B2B2C structures.

Market Sizing & TAM/SAM/SOM Analysis

Rigorous, data-driven market analysis to define your opportunity and validate your strategy. We conduct comprehensive TAM (Total Addressable Market), SAM (Serviceable Available Market), and SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market) analysis using multiple methodologies including top-down, bottom-up, and value-theory approaches. Our analysis identifies the most valuable market segments, quantifies market growth drivers, and provides the robust market sizing that investors expect. We have deep expertise in emerging climate tech markets where traditional data sources are limited.

Competitive Landscape Mapping

Comprehensive competitive intelligence to inform your strategic positioning and identify opportunities for differentiation. We map both direct and indirect competitors, analyze their business models, funding history, go-to-market strategies, and competitive advantages. Our analysis reveals white space opportunities, potential partnership targets, and threats on the horizon. We help you articulate a clear, defensible competitive position that resonates with customers and investors. Our climate tech expertise means we understand the unique competitive dynamics in cleantech markets.

Investor & Grant Research

Strategic research to identify the right funding sources for your venture. We research and qualify VCs, corporate investors, family offices, and strategic partners who have demonstrated interest in your sector, stage, and geography. We also identify relevant government grants, climate funds, and innovation prizes. Our research goes beyond simple lists—we provide insights on investment thesis, portfolio fit, check sizes, and decision-making processes to help you prioritize and tailor your outreach for maximum success.

Client Research

Deep customer analysis to validate product-market fit and inform strategic decisions. We conduct primary and secondary research to understand target customer segments, their pain points, buying behaviors, and willingness to pay. Our research includes competitive alternative analysis, customer journey mapping, and value proposition testing. We help you identify early adopters, understand decision-making units in enterprise sales, and develop customer acquisition strategies. Our work provides the customer insights needed to refine your product roadmap and go-to-market strategy.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Data-driven strategies that translate market insights into actionable plans for customer acquisition and growth. We help you define target customer segments, prioritize beachhead markets, select optimal sales channels, and develop pricing strategies. Our GTM strategies include customer acquisition cost analysis, lifetime value modeling, and sales process design. We understand the unique challenges of climate tech GTM including longer sales cycles, regulatory considerations, and the importance of strategic pilots and partnerships.

Pitch Deck Creation & Review

Develop investor-grade pitch decks that tell your story compellingly and withstand due diligence scrutiny. We create decks from scratch or refine existing presentations, ensuring they follow best practices in structure, narrative flow, and data visualization. Our decks are backed by rigorous analysis and tailored to your specific investor audience—whether pre-seed angels, Series A VCs, or corporate investors. We provide detailed speaker notes and help you prepare for common investor questions. Our deep understanding of what climate tech investors look for gives you a competitive edge.
